Live Field Operations 📍

GroundHalo

Real-time canvass tracking for political campaigns and field operations. Directors see their team on a live map. Workers clock in with one tap.

  • Live GPS tracking with 5-minute pings
  • Start & end shift with one tap
  • Director dashboard with field map
  • iOS & Android native app
Visit groundhalo.com →

Lemonade Class

A classroom micro-business simulation for grades 6–7. Students run a lemonade stand and learn real financial literacy through hands-on decisions.

  • Pricing, production & marketing decisions
  • Revenue vs. profit, fixed vs. variable costs
  • Inventory management & waste tracking
  • iPad-ready for the classroom
